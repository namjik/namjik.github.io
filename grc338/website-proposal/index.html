About

[image of the interior of cafe]

Haru is a modern, casual, and simple-style cafe in Seoul, specifically located in Hongdae, from South Korea. We serve fresh basked desserts, tasty coffee, and other drinks.
The cafe name ‘Haru’ means a day in Korean. Just like the name of our cafe, we would like everyone to enjoy and feel free to visit our cafe every day.

[image of coffee]

Attention to every detail From harvesting to packaging and brewing, we give attention to every detail so that the vibrant color, Umami-rich flavor and nutrition is preserved at its best.
