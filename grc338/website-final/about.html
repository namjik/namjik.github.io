About

Haru is a modern, casual, and simple-style cafe based in Seoul, specifically located in Hongdae, from South Korea. We serve fresh basked desserts, tasty coffee, and other drinks. We now have location in San Luis Obispo, CA at the momtent. We are excited to open up in a new area!

The cafe name ‘Haru’ means a day in Korean. Just like the name of our cafe, we would like everyone to enjoy and feel free to visit our cafe every day.

Attention to every detail

From harvesting to packaging and brewing, we give attention to every detail so that the vibrant color, Umami-rich flavor and nutrition is preserved at its best.
